About the author

Pam Kovacek

Documentary researcher and nomadic culture advocate, raised between Ulaanbaatar and the Arkhangai aimag.

When not recording family histories across the steppe, Pam helps fund community-led archives and teaches visiting artists how to read the sky for weather.
